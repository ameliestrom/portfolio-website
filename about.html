My Story

I’m Amelie. A UI Designer with a background in fashion retail management, and marketing. My passion is creating designs that make apps meaningful, accessible and intuitive. I can spend hours discussing design, and brainstorming solutions to problems that arise during my projects. Born in Sweden, grown up in Switzerland, and developed my skills and experience in London, San Francisco, Australia, and Japan, I would like to call myself a world explorer. I’m currently based in Provence, South of France and I’ve been spending my 2020 working and studying remotely from here. I love traveling, and exploring cultures by meeting, and connecting with new people.
